Best Possible Mushroom Tea Recipe

+ Grind dry mushrooms to a powder with an electric coffee grinder
+ Bring half a cup of water per dose to boil
+ Remove from heat
+ Stir in the powder (optionally add some fruit tea for better taste)
+ Stir occasionally for 5 minutes
+ Strain the tea
+ Add half a cup of cold water or ice cubes to bring the temperature down

Enjoy!
