MEAT LUMP

Mix together:

1 lb lean ground beef
1/2 cup rolled oats (not instant)
1 small sweet onion, diced
1 clove garlic, minced
1/3 cup catsup
1 egg, lightly beaten
1 tsp salt
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp thyme

Place mixture in a 1 quart loaf pan.
Sprinkle lightly with brown sugar.
Add and spread additional catsup for a thin glaze.

Bake 50 to 55 minutes at 350F.
Let sit for 5 to 10 minutes before cutting.
